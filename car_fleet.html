Overview Medic of the Week Car Fleet Top Five Hours

Car Fleet

Paramedic - Fiat Ducato

Top Speed: 92 Mph / 148 km/h

The Fiat Ducato is a spacious and versatile van, ideal for our ambulance fleet. It is known for its durability and reliability on long-distance calls.

Paramedic - A45

The Mercedes-AMG A45 has a top speed of 168 mph. It goes from 0 to 62 mph in 3.9 seconds. The A45 is a high-performance version of the A-Class hatchback with a 2.0-liter, 4-cylinder turbo engine that produces 415 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It also has an 8-speed dual-clutch transmission and all-wheel drive.

Advanced Paramedic - Cupra Formentor Custom

The new flagship model, which delivers high performance thanks to the 2.0-litre TSI 310PS 4Drive engine, accelerates from 0-62mph in just 4.9 seconds. It includes a Digital Cockpit, LED headlights, and a leather steering wheel as standard.

Doctor - C63s CSL

With a 4.0-litre V8, the C63s CSL produces an incredible 375kW of power and 700Nm of torque, while achieving a fuel economy claim of just 8.6 litres per 100km. It goes from 0-100km/h in 4.0 seconds.

Human Resources - RS6

The RS6 accelerates from 0 to 62 mph in 3.4 seconds. It has an impressive torque of 850 Nm, making it a fast and reliable vehicle for HR-related tasks.

Directors - RS7

The RS7 offers the best of both worlds: a stylish coupé-like design with the brute force of the V8 TFSI quattro engine. It accelerates from 0-62 mph in 3.4 seconds and has a torque of 850 Nm.

Chief Ambulance Officer - RSQ8

With 600 PS of power, the RSQ8 is the most powerful SUV coupé from Audi Sport. It accelerates from 0-62 mph in 3.8 seconds and has a system torque of 800 Nm.

Chief Executive Officer - GLE 900

The GLE 900 boasts 662 kW / 900 hp of power and a torque of 1250 Nm. It accelerates from 0-100 km/h in 3.2 seconds and has a top speed of 205 mph / 330 km/h.
