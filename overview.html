Welcome to the Overview of LDN Ambulance Service

What is LAS?

The London Ambulance Service (LAS) is a vital organization dedicated to providing emergency medical care to those in need. Our service is crucial to the safety and well-being of London’s residents and visitors.

What is this website for?

This website serves as the central hub for the London Ambulance Service, providing important information for applicants, current staff, and the public. It includes sections on the organization’s mission, services, and resources available to members and the public.

How do I apply to join LAS?

We encourage individuals with or without medical experience to apply to join LAS. Our website provides all the necessary information and a portal to submit your application.

How do I become a Medic of the Week?

Medics are chosen for their exceptional work in the field, often going above and beyond in their service. Each week, one medic is selected to be featured.
