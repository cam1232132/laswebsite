What is the LAS?

The LDN Ambulance Service (LAS) is a dynamic and highly skilled team dedicated to providing essential pre-hospital emergency medical services. We operate in a fast-paced, high-pressure environment, always ready to respond to emergencies with professionalism and care.

What is this website for?

This website is designed to inform the public about the LDN Ambulance Service, provide details about our operations, team members, fleet, and to answer any frequently asked questions. It also features the Medic of the Week and Top Five Hours, allowing us to highlight outstanding contributions from our team members.
