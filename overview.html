What is the LAS?

The LDN Ambulance Service (LAS) is the emergency ambulance service that operates in LDN. Responsibilities: The LAS responds to urgent and emergency medical situations, including 111 calls. They provide free healthcare to patients and work with hospitals and other emergency services. Staff The LAS has over 30 dedicated employees and has 6 leadership team members.
