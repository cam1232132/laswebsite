Welcome to the LDN Ambulance Service

What is the LAS?

The LDN Ambulance Service is a dedicated team providing emergency medical care. We are a volunteer-based ambulance service, focused on saving lives and providing quality healthcare on the front lines.

What is this website for?

This website serves as the official online presence for the LDN Ambulance Service. Here, you can find important information about our fleet, medic of the week, top hours, and frequently asked questions.
