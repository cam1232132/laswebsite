Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply?

Apply via Discord at the provided link.

How long does the application process take?

The application process typically takes about 72 hours.

How many hours are required per week?

A minimum of 5 hours per week is required, and more hours can aid in your progression.

Can you join without previous medical experience?

Yes, everyone is given the opportunity to apply and participate in a training session, regardless of prior experience.

How long will training take?

Training typically lasts 1 to 2 hours, depending on your ability to learn and the trainer's judgment.

When can I be trained?

Training occurs every Sunday at 3PM, with potential additional sessions if you cannot make it.

How do I apply?

Apply via this Discord link.

How much does LAS pay?

Pay varies by rank: Student paramedic (£6,000), Newly qualified paramedic (£7,500), Paramedic (£9,000), Advanced Paramedic (£11,000), Doctor (£11,750), HR+ disclosed on request.

Can you be accepted with a criminal record?

No, LAS requires a clean DBS check and does not accept applicants with a criminal record or gang affiliations.

How does joining LAS work?

After applying, you will receive a response within 72 hours. If accepted, you'll undergo training before being ready to work in the field.

Should I join LAS?

Yes, joining LAS offers great work friends, experience, professional growth, pride, and a competitive salary.
